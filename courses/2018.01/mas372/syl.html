Course Description

This course in an introduction to the mathematical theory of statistics, including sampling distributions, estimation, hypothesis testing, linear models and multivariate distributions.

Prerequisites

  • MAS 371

Text and Materials

  • Rice, Mathematical Statistics and Data Analysis, 3rd edition
