Course Description

Finite mathematics is an introduction to mathematical techniques used in business, economics, and other areas. Topics include linear functions, linear systems of equations, matrices, linear programming, interest and probability.

Prerequisites and Requirements Met

There are no prerequisites beyond high school algebra skills. This course meets the Liberal Studies Area 4 requirement of College's General Education Program.

Text and Materials

  • Lial et al., Finite Mathematics with Applications, 11th edition
