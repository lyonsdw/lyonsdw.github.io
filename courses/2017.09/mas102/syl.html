Course Description

In order to prepare for a first course in calculus, we review the basic theory and applications of polynomial, rational, exponential, logarithmic and trigonometric functions.

Prerequisites and Requirements Met

There are no prerequisites beyond high school algebra skills. This course does not satisfy any requirements in the General Education Program or the Constellation LVC program.

Text and Materials

  • Connally et al., Functions Modeling Change, 5th edition
