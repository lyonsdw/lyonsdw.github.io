Course Description

We extend the theory and applications of differentiation and integration, developed in introductory calculus courses, to functions involving more variables.

Prerequisites

MAS 112 or MAS 162, or permission by the instructor

Text and Materials

  • McCallum et al., Calculus, Multivariable, 6th edition
