Learning Outcomes and Course Objectives

David W. Lyons
Spring 2012

A successful student learning experience includes progress towards the following.
  • Understanding course material
  • Critical reading
  • Analytical thinking
  • Clear, concise writing

The most closely related objectives from the IDEA course evaluation instrument are these.

  • Gaining factual knowledge (terminology, classifications, methods, trends)
  • Learning fundamental principles, generalizations, or theories
  • Learning to apply course material (to improve thinking, problem solving, and decisions)
