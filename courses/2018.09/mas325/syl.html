Course Description

In the spirit of Felix Klein's Erlanger Programm, this course surveys Mobius, hyperbolic, elliptic, projective and absolute geometries as compared to and contrasted with Euclidean geometry.

Prerequisites

MAS 202 and MAS 222

Text and Materials

  • Michael Henle, Modern Geometries, 2nd edition
