Course Description

This course in an introduction to the mathematical theory of probability, including discrete and continuous probability distributions.

Prerequisites

  • MAS 202

Text and Materials

  • Rice, Mathematical Statistics and Data Analysis, 3rd edition
  • Lyons, Supplementary Notes
