IDEA Survey Details

David W. Lyons
Fall 2013

updated 6 November

The Individual Development and Educational Assessment Center (IDEA) opinion survey is based on a list of twelve objectives (see p.2 of the linked document). Of the twelve, the three most applicable are the following.
  • Gaining factual knowledge (terminology, classifications, methods, trends)
  • Learning fundamental principles, generalizations, or theories
  • Learning to apply course material (to improve thinking, problem solving, and decisions)
