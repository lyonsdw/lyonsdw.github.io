Author Biography About the author

David W. Lyons is a professor of mathematics at Lebanon Valley College in Annville, Pennsylvania, USA, where he has taught and conducted research since 2000. Lyons works in mathematical physics, leading a student-faculty research program in quantum information science since 2002. For more information, visit his academic website 2 .
