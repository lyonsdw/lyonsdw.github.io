Instructor's solution for Checkpoint 3.3.6.

By Lemma 3.3.5, fixed points are symmetric with respect to the unit circle \(C\text{.}\) By Exercise 3.2.6.6, we have \(z^{\ast C} = \frac{1}{z^\ast}\text{.}\) From this we have \(|z||z^{\ast C}|=1\text{,}\) so the norms of \(|z|,|z^{\ast}\) are either both 1, or one of the norms is less than 1 and the other is greater. Finally, a single (degenerate) fixed point must have norm 1.
