  1. Let \(T(a,b)\) denote the matrix \(\twotwo{a}{b}{-b}{a}\) in \({\mathcal M}_\C\text{.}\) It is straightforward to check that \(T(a,b)+T(c,d)=T(a+c,b+d)\) and \(T(a,b)T(c,d)=T(ac-bd,ad+bc)\text{.}\)
  2. Let \(z=a+bi\) and \(w=c+di\) so that \(M(z)=T(a,b)\) and \(M(w)=T(c,d)\text{.}\) Again it is straightforward to check that \(M(z)+M(w)=M(z+w)\) and \(M(z)M(w)=M(zw)\) (to see the last equality, just notice that \(zw=(a+bi)(c+di)=(ac-bd)+(bc+ad)i\)).
