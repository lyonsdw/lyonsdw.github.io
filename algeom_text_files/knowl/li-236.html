Item 2.5.3.6.f.
Let \(s\colon S^2 \to \extC\) denote the sterographic projection (see (1.3.6)). Show that the map \(\Proj(\C^2)\to S^2\) given by \([(\alpha,\beta)]\to s^{-1}(\alpha/\beta)\) if \(\beta\neq 0\) and given by \([(\alpha,\beta)]\to (0,0,1)\) if \(\beta=0\) is well-defined and is a bijection.
