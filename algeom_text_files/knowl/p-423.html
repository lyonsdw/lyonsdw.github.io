We have

\begin{align*} S\amp \leftrightarrow \twotwo{0}{1}{1}{-p}\\ S^{-1}\amp \leftrightarrow \twotwo{-p}{-1}{-1}{0}\sim \twotwo{p}{1}{1}{0} \end{align*}

so \(S^{-1}w=\frac{pw+1}{w}\text{,}\) so \(S^{-1}\) takes \(0,\beta,\infty\) to \(\infty,\frac{p\beta+1}{\beta},p\text{.}\) Thus, \(S^{-1}\) transforms the line through \(0,\beta\) in the \(w\)-plane to the line through \(\frac{p\beta+1}{\beta},p\) in the \(z\)-plane. This degenerate Steiner line has a direction vector given by \(\frac{p\beta+1}{\beta}-p=\frac{p\beta+1-p\beta}{\beta} =\frac{1}{\beta} = \frac{\beta^\ast}{|\beta|^2}\propto \beta^\ast\text{,}\) as claimed.
