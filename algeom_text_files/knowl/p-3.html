Elements in the set of complex numbers \(\C\) are in one-to-one correspondence with points in the 2-dimensional real plane \(\R^2\) (where \(\R\) denotes the set of real numbers). We will write \(z\leftrightarrow (x,y)\) to denote that the complex number \(z\) corresponds to the ordered pair \((x,y)\) of real numbers.
