Item 3.3.6.1.b.
Prove (3.3.2) verifying the following. Starting with a transformation of the form (3.3.1), show that the substitution \(a=\frac{e^{it/2}}{\sqrt{1-|z_0|^2}}, b=\frac{-e^{it/2}z_0}{\sqrt{1-|z_0|^2}}\) leads to a transformation of the form (3.3.2). Conversely, starting with (3.3.2), setting \(t=2\arg a, z_0=-\frac{b}{a}\) leads to (3.3.1).
