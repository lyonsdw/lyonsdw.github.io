Proposition 3.5.4.

Given two points \(P=[u],P'=[u']\) in \(\R\Proj_2\text{,}\) there is a unique projective line \(\overline{PP'}=[u\times u']\) that contains them. Given two lines \(\ell=[n],\ell'=[n']\) in \(\R\Proj_2\text{,}\) there is a unique projective point \([n\times n']\) in their intersection \(\ell\cap \ell'\text{.}\)
