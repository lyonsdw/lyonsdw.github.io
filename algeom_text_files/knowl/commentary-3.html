  1. Let \(T(a,b)\) denote the matrix \(\twotwo{a}{b}{-b}{a}\) in \({\mathcal M}_\C\text{.}\) We have the following.
    \begin{align*} T(a,b)+T(c,d)\amp = \twotwo{a}{b}{-b}{a} + \twotwo{c}{d}{-d}{c} \\ \amp = \twotwo{a+c}{b+d}{-b-d}{a+c}\\ \amp = T(a+c,b+d)\\ T(a,b)T(c,d)\amp = \twotwo{a}{b}{-b}{a} \twotwo{c}{d}{-d}{c} \\ \amp =\twotwo{ac-bd}{ad+bc}{-ad-bc}{ac-bd}\\ \amp = T(ac-bd,ad+bc) \end{align*}
  2. Let \(z=a+bi\) and \(w=c+di\) so that \(M(z)=T(a,b)\) and \(M(w)=T(c,d)\text{.}\) We have the following.
    \begin{align*} C(M(z)+M(w)) \amp = C(T(a,b)+T(c,d))\\ \amp = C(T(a+c,b+d)) \;\;(\text{by part (a)})\\ \amp = (a+c)+i(b+d)\\ \amp = (a+ib)+(c+id)\\ \amp =z+w\\ C(M(z)M(w)) \amp = C(T(a,b)T(c,d))\\ \amp = C(T(ac-bd,ad+bc)) \;\;(\text{by part (a)})\\ \amp = (ac-bd)+i(ad+bc)\\ \amp = (a+ib)(c+id)\\ \amp =z+w \end{align*}
