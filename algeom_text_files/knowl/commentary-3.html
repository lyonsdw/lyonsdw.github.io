Instructor's solution for Checkpoint 1.2.2.

(This is similar to the solution to Exercise 1.1.7.12.) Let \(T(u,v)\) denote the matrix \(\twotwo{u}{v}{-v^\ast}{u^\ast}\) in \({\mathcal M}_\C\text{.}\) It is straightforward to check that \(T(u,v)+T(r,s)=T(u+v,r+x)\) and \(T(u,v)T(r,s)=T(ur-vs^\ast,us+vr^\ast)\text{.}\)
