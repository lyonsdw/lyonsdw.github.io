Proposition 1.3.8. Characterization of normal subgroups.

Let \(K\) be a subgroup of a group \(G\text{.}\) The following are equivalent. 1 

  1. \(K\) is a normal subgroup of \(G\)
  2. \(K\) is the kernel of some group homomorphism \(\phi\colon G\to H\)
  3. the multiplication (1.3.1) of cosets of \(K\) is well-defined
  4. gK = Kg for all \(g\in G\)
  5. \(gkg^{-1}\in K\) for all \(k\in K\text{,}\) \(g\in G\)
