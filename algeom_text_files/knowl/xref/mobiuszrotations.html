Exercise 4.
Let \(R_{Z,\theta}\colon S^2\to S^2\) and \(T_{Z,\theta}\colon \extC\to \extC\) be given by
\begin{align*} R_{Z,\theta}(a,b,c)\amp = (a\cos\theta-b\sin\theta,a\sin\theta+b\cos\theta,c)\\ T_{Z,\theta}(z)\amp = \begin{cases} e^{i\theta}z \amp z\neq \infty\\ \infty\amp z=\infty.\end{cases} \end{align*}
Show that \(R_{Z,\theta},T_{Z,\theta}\) are conjugate transformations with respect to stereographic projection.
