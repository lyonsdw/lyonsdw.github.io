Item c.
We need \(\alpha,\beta\) such that \((1,0)\to \alpha(c,1)\text{,}\) \((0,1)\to \beta(b,1)\) and \((1,1)\to \alpha(a,1)\text{.}\) Solve that system to get \((\alpha,\beta)\propto (a-b,c-a)\text{.}\) This leads to the projective transformation given by the matrix \(\twotwo{(a-b)c}{(c-a)b}{a-b}{c-a}\text{.}\)
