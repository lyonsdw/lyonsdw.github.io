We wish to show that the map \(G\to \Perm({\mathcal F}(X,Y))\) given by \(g\to [\alpha\to \alpha\circ g^{-1}]\) is a homomorphism. Indeed, for \(g,h\in G\) and \(\alpha\in {\mathcal F}(X,Y)\text{,}\) we have

\begin{equation*} (gh)\alpha = \alpha\circ (gh)^{-1}= \alpha\circ (h^{-1}g^{-1}) = (\alpha\circ h^{-1})\circ g^{-1}=g(h(\alpha)). \end{equation*}
