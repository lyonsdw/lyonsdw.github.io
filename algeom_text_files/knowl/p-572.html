Let \(v_1,v_2,\ldots,v_{n+1}\) be an independent set of vectors in \(\F^{n+1}\) and let \(v_0=\sum_{i=1}^{n+1}c_iv_i\) for some nonzero scalars \(c_1,c_2,\ldots,c_{n+1}\text{.}\) There exists a unique projective transformation that maps \([e_i]\to [v_i]\) for \(0\leq i\leq n+1\text{.}\)
