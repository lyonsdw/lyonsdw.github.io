  1. Start with the inverse of the desired transformation, then take the inverse using matrix methods.
    \begin{align*} [a\to 1, b\to 0,c\to \infty] \amp = \left[z\to \frac{z-b}{z-c}\frac{a-c}{a-b}\right]\\ \amp \leftrightarrow \twotwo{a-c}{b(c-a)}{a-b}{c(b-a)}\\ [a\to 1, b\to 0,c\to \infty]^{-1} \amp \leftrightarrow \twotwo{c(b-a)}{b(a-c)}{b-a}{a-c} \\ [1\to a, 0\to b,\infty\to c] \amp = \left[z\to \frac{c(b-a)z +b(a-c)}{(b-a)z+a-c} \right] \end{align*}
  2. Put together the pieces from the previous part.
