Instructor's solution for Exercise 2.5.3.5.

Let \(x_0\in X\) and consider the function \(f_{x_0}\colon G\to X\) given by \(f_{x_0}(g)= gx_0\text{.}\) Applying Fact 1.4.3 to \(f_{x_0}\text{,}\) we have \(G/\!\!\sim_{f_{x_0}}=G/\Stab(x_0)\text{,}\) and \(f_{x_0}(G)=\Orb(x_0)\text{.}\)

Alternatively, it is straightforward to check that the given map is well-defined, one-to-one, and onto.
