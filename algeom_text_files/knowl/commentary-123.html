Instructor's solution for Exercise 3.3.6.2.

Let \(Tz=\frac{z-p}{1-p^\ast z}\text{,}\) so that \(Tp=0\text{.}\) Let \(L\) be the diameter that passes through \(Tp,Tq\text{.}\) Then \(T^{-1}(L)\) is a hyperbolic straight line that contains \(p,q\text{.}\) Uniqueness is a direct consequence of Checkpoint 3.3.7.
