It is straightforward to solve \(w=\frac{az+b}{cz+d}\) for \(z\text{.}\) We get \(z=\frac{dw-b}{-cw+a}\text{.}\) When we check the composition, we see that

\begin{equation*} \frac{a\left(\frac{dw-b}{-cw+a}\right)+b}{c\left(\frac{dw-b}{-cw+a}\right)+d} = \cdots = w\frac{ad-bc}{ad-bc}. \end{equation*}

Thus it is clear that the condition \(ad-bc\) is necessary and sufficient for invertibility.
