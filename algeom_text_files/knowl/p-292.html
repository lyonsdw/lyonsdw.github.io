We showed in Exercise 2.4.2.11 that the composition of isomorphisms is an isomorphism, so the group operation is well defined. The identity is an isomorphism, and we showed in Exercise 2.4.2.4 that the inverse of an isomorphism is an isomorphism. Associativity is a property of function composition. All of the requirements of a group hold for \(\Aut(G)\text{.}\)
