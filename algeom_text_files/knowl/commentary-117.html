Let \(Tz=\frac{z-p}{1-p^\ast z}\text{,}\) so that \(Tp=0\text{.}\) There is only one hyperbolic straight line that contains \(Tp,Tq\text{,}\) namely, the diameter line of the unit circle, say \(L\text{,}\) that passes through \(Tq\text{.}\) Therefore \(T^{-1}(L)\) is the unique hyperbolic straight line that contains \(p,q\text{.}\)
