Let \(Tz=\frac{az+b}{cz+d}\) and \(Sz=\frac{ez+f}{gz+h}\text{.}\) Then we have

\begin{align*} S(Tz)\amp = \frac{e\frac{az+b}{cz+d} +f}{g\frac{az+b}{cz+d}+h}\\ \amp = \frac{e(az+b)+f(cz+d)}{g(az+b)+h(cz+d)}\\ \amp = \frac{(ea+fc)z + (eb+fd)}{(ga+hc)z+(gb+hd)}\\ \amp = \frac{rz+s}{tz+u} \end{align*}

for \(r=ea+fc\text{,}\) \(s=eb+fd\text{,}\) \(t=ga+hc\text{,}\) \(u=gb+hd\text{.}\) The composition is invertible because both of its factors are invertible. It follows from the previous exercise that \(ru-ts\neq 0\text{.}\)
