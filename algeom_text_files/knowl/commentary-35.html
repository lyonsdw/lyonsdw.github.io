  1. Commutative: \(S^1,C_n,\Z,\Z_n,\F^\ast\text{.}\) Not commutative: \(\Perm(X),D_n,GL(n),U(\Quat)\)
  2. Let \(n\gt 2\text{.}\) Let \(\sigma=[3,1,2]\times \Id,\tau=[2,1,3]\times \Id\) be elements of \(S_n\) that are the same as \(\sigma,\tau\) defined in Checkpoint 2.1.2 on \(\{1,2,3\}\subseteq \{1,2,\ldots,n\}\text{,}\) and identity on \(\{4,5,\ldots,n\}\text{.}\) Then we have \([1,3,2]\times \Id = \sigma\tau\neq \tau\sigma= [3,2,1]\text{.}\)
