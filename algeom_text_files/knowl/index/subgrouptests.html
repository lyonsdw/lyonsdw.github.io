Proposition 2.3.4. Subgroup tests.

Let \(H\) be a subset of a group \(G\text{.}\) The following are equivalent.
  1. \(H\) is a subgroup of \(G\)
  2. (2-step subgroup test) \(H\) is nonempty, \(ab\) is in \(H\) for every \(a,b\) in \(H\text{,}\) and \(a^{-1}\) is in \(H\) for every \(a\) in \(H\)
  3. (1-step subgroup test) \(H\) is nonempty and \(ab^{-1}\) is in \(H\) for every \(a,b\) in \(H\)
