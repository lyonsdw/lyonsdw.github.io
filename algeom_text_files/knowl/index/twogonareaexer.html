Exercise 3. Area of an elliptic 2-gon.
An elliptic 2-gon is a figure with two vertices connected by two elliptic line segments. In \(\extC\text{,}\) any 2-gon is congruent to a set of the form \(R_{\alpha} :=\{z\in \C\colon 0\leq \arg z \leq \alpha\}\cup \{\infty\}\) for some \(\alpha\) in the range \(0\leq \alpha\lt 2\pi\text{.}\) See FigureÂ 3.4.25. Use an area integral in \(\extC\) to show that the area of \(R_{\alpha}\) is \(2\alpha\text{.}\) Verify that this is the right answer using a picture of \(S^2\text{.}\)
