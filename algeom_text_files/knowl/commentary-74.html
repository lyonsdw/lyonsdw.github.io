One example is complex conjugation on \(\C^\ast\text{.}\) Any nontrivial automorphism of an Abelian group is an example, because conjugation by any element is the identity. So, for example, \(\mu_2\colon \Z_5\to \Z_5\) given by \(\mu_2(x)=2x\) works.
