Let \(H\) be a subset of a group \(G\text{.}\) The following are equivalent.

  1. \(H\) is a subgroup of \(G\)
  2. (2-step subgroup test) \(ab\) is in \(H\) for every \(a,b\) in \(H\) (\(H\) is closed under the group operation) and \(a^{-1}\) is in \(H\) for every \(a\) in \(H\) (\(H\) is closed under group inversion)
  3. (1-step subgroup test) \(ab^{-1}\) is in \(H\) for every \(a,b\) in \(H\)
