Instructor’s solution for Checkpoint 1.2.2.

(This is similar to the solution to Exercise 1.1.7.12.) Let \(T(u,v)\) denote the matrix \(\twotwo{u}{v}{-v^\ast}{u^\ast}\) in \({\mathcal M}_\Quat\text{.}\) We have the following.
\begin{align*} T(u,v)+T(r,s)\amp = \twotwo{u}{v}{-v^\ast}{u^\ast} + \twotwo{r}{s}{-s^\ast}{r^\ast} \\ \amp = \twotwo{u+r}{v+s}{-(v+s)^\ast}{(u+r)^\ast}\\ \amp = T(u+r,v+s)\\ T(u,v)T(r,s)\amp = \twotwo{u}{v}{-v^\ast}{u^\ast} \twotwo{r}{s}{-s^\ast}{r^\ast} \\ \amp = \twotwo{ur-vs^\ast}{us+vr^\ast}{-(us+vr^\ast)^\ast}{(ur-vs^\ast)^\ast}\\ \amp = T(ur-vs^\ast,us+vr^\ast) \end{align*}
