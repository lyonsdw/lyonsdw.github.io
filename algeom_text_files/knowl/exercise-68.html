Exercise 7. Isomorphic images of generators are generators.

Suppose that a group \(G\) is generated by a set \(S\text{,}\) so that \(G=\langle S\rangle\text{.}\) Let \(\phi\colon G\to H\) be an isomorphism of groups, and let \(\phi(S)=\{\phi(s)\colon s\in S\}\text{.}\) Show that \(H=\langle \phi(S)\rangle\text{.}\)
