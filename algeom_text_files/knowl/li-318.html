Item b.

Let \(\omega = e^{i2\pi/3}\) and let \(Tz=(z,\omega,\omega^2,\omega^3=1)\text{.}\) We have the following.
\begin{align*} Tz\amp =\frac{z-\omega^2}{z-\omega^3}\frac{\omega-\omega^3}{\omega-\omega^2}\\ \amp = (1+\omega)\left(\frac{z-\omega^2}{z-1}\right)\\ T \amp \leftrightarrow \twotwo{1+\omega}{-\omega^2-1}{1}{-1}\\ T^{-1} \amp \leftrightarrow \twotwo{-1}{\omega^2+1}{-1}{1+\omega} \end{align*}
We have
\begin{align*} z^{\ast C} \amp = T^{-1}((Tz)^\ast)\\ \amp = T^{-1} \left(\frac{(1+\omega^2)z^\ast -\omega-1}{z^\ast-1}\right)\\ \amp = \frac{-[(1+\omega^2)z^\ast -\omega-1] +(z-1)(\omega^2+1)}{-[(1+\omega^2)z^\ast -\omega-1] +(1+\omega)(z^\ast-1)}\\ \amp = \cdots = \frac{1}{z^\ast} \end{align*}
