Instructor's solution for Proposition 3.3.13.

Let \(z_1,z_2\in \D\text{,}\) and let \(q_1,q_2\) be points on the hyperbolic line through \(z_1,z_2\) that lie on the unit circle, with \(q_i\) "on the \(z_i\) side" for \(i=1,2\text{,}\) that is, \(q_i=T^{-1}z_i\text{,}\) where \(T\) is the transformation found in Checkpoint 3.3.10. Be invariance of the cross ratio under Möbius transformations, we have

\begin{equation*} d(z_1,z_2)=\ln(z_1,z_2,q_2,q_1)=\ln(Tz_1,Tz_2,Tq_2,Tq_1)=d(Tz_1,Tz_2). \end{equation*}
