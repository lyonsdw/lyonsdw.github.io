Note on reading exercises versus end-of-section exercises. In the narrative for each section, links labeled "Checkpoint" and "Proof" are meant to be reading exercises, that is, part of the process of active reading. Working through end-of-section exercises is meant to take place after reading and reading exercises. In a seminar style class, where most of class time is spent with students presenting solutions, the recommended order for class discussion is: (1) questions about the reading; (2) solutions to reading exercises; and (3) solutions to end-of-section exercises.
