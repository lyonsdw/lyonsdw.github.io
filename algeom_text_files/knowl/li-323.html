Item a.

The fixed points of \(T\) are \(\pm 1\text{,}\) so the normal form is
\begin{equation*} \frac{Tz-1}{Tz+1}=\lambda\frac{z-1}{z+1}. \end{equation*}
Putting \(z=2\) and solving for \(\lambda\text{,}\) we get \(\lambda=-1\text{.}\) The Steiner circles of the first kind are circles through \(\pm 1\) (and the real axis), and Steiner circles of the second kind are orthogonal to the first family (including the imaginary axis).
