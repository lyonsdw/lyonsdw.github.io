Let \(T\) be the transformation \(T(z)=e^{-i\arg(u-v)}(z-v)\text{.}\) \(T\) is conformal because \(T\) is a composition of a rotation and a translation, so we have \(\angle uvw= \angle T(u)T(v)T(w)\text{.}\) Since \(T(u)\) is on the positive real axis and \(T(u)=0\text{,}\) we have
\begin{align*} \angle T(u)T(v)T(w)\amp =\arg(T(w))\\ \amp =\arg(e^{-i\arg(u-v)}(w-v))\\ \amp =\arg(w-v)-\arg(u-v)\\ \amp =\arg\left(\frac{w-v}{u-v}\right) \end{align*}
as desired.
