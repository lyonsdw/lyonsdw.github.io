Definition 3.3.1.

Let \(\D=\{z\colon |z|\lt 1\}\) denote the open unit disk in the complex plane. The hyerbolic group, denoted \(\H\), is the subgroup of the Möbius group \(\M\) of transformations that map \(\D\) onto itself. The pair \((\D,\H)\) is the (Poincaré) disk model of hyperbolic geometry.
