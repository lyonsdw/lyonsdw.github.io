Proposition 1.3.3. Properties of homomorphisms.

Let \(\phi\colon G\to H\) be a homomorphism of groups. Let \(e_G,e_H\) denote the identity elements of \(G,H\text{,}\) respectively. We have the following.

\begin{align*} \phi(e_G) \amp = e_H\\ \phi\left(g^{-1}\right) \amp = \left(\phi(g)\right)^{-1} \text{ for all } g\in G \end{align*}
