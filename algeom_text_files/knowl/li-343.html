Item b.

Rearranging the equation for \(z'=z^{\ast C}\) in part iii, we have \((z-a)(z'-a)=r^2\text{.}\) Taking the norm of both sides, we have \(|z-a||z'-a|=r^2\text{.}\) Taking the argument of both sides, we have \(\arg(z'-a)-\arg(z-a)=0\text{.}\) This implies that \(z,z'\) lie on the same ray emanating from \(a\text{.}\)
