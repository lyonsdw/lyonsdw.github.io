Hint 2.2.2.6.1.
For the last part, observe that \((a,b)\in \Z_n\times \Z_m\) is a generator if and only if every entry in the sequence
\begin{equation*} (a,b),(2a,2b),(3a,3b),\ldots,(nma,nmb) \end{equation*}
is distinct. Consider \((La,Lb)\text{,}\) where \(L\) is the least common multiple of \(n,m\text{.}\)
