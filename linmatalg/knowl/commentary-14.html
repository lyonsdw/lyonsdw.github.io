Instructor’s solution for Exercise 4.3.4.

The given formula is just the summation notation version of (3.11) applied to (4.5).
\begin{equation*} \mbox{$i,j$ entry of $[D(f\circ g)(\mathbf{t}_0)]$} = (\mbox{$i$th row of $[Df(\mathbf{x}_0)]$}) \cdot (\mbox{$j$th column of $[Dg(\mathbf{t}_0)]$}) \end{equation*}
