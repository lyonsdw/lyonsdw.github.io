Instructor’s solution for Exercise 1.4.3 (verification of (1.2)).

We have
\begin{align*} \mathbf{e}_i\cdot \mathbf{x} \amp = (0,0,\ldots,0,1,0,\ldots,0) \cdot (x_1,x_2,\ldots,x_n) \amp \text{(defn. of standard basis vect.)}\\ \amp = 0x_1+0x_2+\cdots 0x_{i-1} + 1x_i+ 0x_{i+1}+\cdots + 0x_n \amp \text{(defn. of dot product)}\\ \amp = x_i. \end{align*}
