Fact 0.3.3. Construction of functions on sets of equivalence classes.

Let \(\sim\) be an equivalence relation on a set \(X\text{,}\) let \(\pi\colon X\to X/\!\!\sim\) denote the map given by \(x\to [x]\text{,}\) and let \(f\colon X\to Y\) be a function. There exists a map \(\overline{f}\colon X/\!\!\sim \to Y\) such that \(\overline{f}([x])=f(x)\) for all \(x\in X\) if and only if \(f\) is constant on equivalence classes.
