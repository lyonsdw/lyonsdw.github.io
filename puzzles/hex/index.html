The Game of Hex

Hex is an elegant game played on a board like this. 
                              ___
                          ___/   \___
            X         ___/   \___/   \___       O
                  ___/   \___/   \___/   \___
              ___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___
          ___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___
      ___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___
  ___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___
 /   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \
 \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/  
     \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   
         \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   
             \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/
                 \___/   \___/   \___/   \___/  
                     \___/   \___/   \___/
            O            \___/   \___/           X
                             \___/
Two opposite sides of the board are labeled X and the other two sides are labeled O (any two symbols will do). Two players choose which will play X and which will play O. Players take turns placing their symbols inside any empty hexagon. The object is to connect your two edges of the board with a continuous chain of your symbols. The player who constructs such a chain wins.
