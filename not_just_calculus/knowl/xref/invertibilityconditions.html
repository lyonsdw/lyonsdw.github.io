Corollary 1.3.4. Characterization of invertibility.

The following conditions are equivalent for a function \(f\colon S\to T\text{.}\)
  1. The inverse relation \(f^{-1}\subseteq T\times S\) is a function \(f^{-1}\colon T\to S\text{.}\)
  2. The function \(f\colon S\to T\) is surjective and injective.
  3. There exists a function \(g\colon T\to S\) such that \(g\of f = \One_S\) and \(f\of g = \One_T\text{.}\)
